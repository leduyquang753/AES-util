About

AES – Advanced encryption algorithm is a military-level algorithm which can keep your data and messages safe. Once encrypted, you can give the thing to anyone and if they don't know the password (the key), they won't be able to know what is its content. So you can assure your rest when the message is in transit to your pal.
The whole page is run fully client-side. Nothing is sent out of your device during the use of this page.
